Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

How We Handle Your Information

Bremray Electrical respects your privacy. Here's what we do with your information.

What We Collect

When you contact us, we collect:

  • Your name and phone number
  • Your email address (if you provide it)
  • Your address (for service calls)
  • Information about your electrical needs

How We Use It

We use your information to:

  • Contact you about your electrical service request
  • Schedule appointments
  • Send invoices and receipts
  • Follow up on completed work
  • Send occasional updates about our services (you can opt out)

Who We Share With

We don't sell your information. Period. We only share it with:

  • Our scheduling software (to book appointments)
  • Our accounting software (for invoicing)
  • City inspectors (when required for permits)

Your Rights

You can:

  • Ask us what information we have about you
  • Request we delete your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Update incorrect information

Website Cookies

Our website uses basic analytics cookies to understand how people use our site. We don't track you across other websites.

Security

We use standard security measures to protect your information. Your data is stored securely and only accessible by authorized personnel.
