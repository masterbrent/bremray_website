Bremray Electrical provides professional electrical services throughout Ypsilanti and Ypsilanti Township. From historic homes in the Normal Park and College Heights neighborhoods to newer developments in the township, we understand the diverse electrical needs of Ypsi properties.

Electrical Services in Ypsilanti

  • EV Charger Installation: As more Ypsilanti residents switch to electric vehicles, we install Level 2 home charging stations for all EV makes and models. We handle permits with the City of Ypsilanti and Ypsilanti Township, and help you navigate DTE rebate programs.
  • Panel Upgrades: Many Ypsilanti homes, particularly in the Historic East Side and Depot Town areas, need electrical panel upgrades. We modernize outdated fuse boxes and upgrade 100-amp services to 200-amp panels for today's electrical demands.
  • Whole House Generators: Don't let power outages disrupt your life. We install Generac automatic backup generators that seamlessly power your Ypsilanti home when the grid goes down.
  • Residential Electrical: From basic outlet repairs to complete home rewiring, we handle all residential electrical needs for Ypsilanti homeowners.
