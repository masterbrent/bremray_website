Electrical Services in Ann Arbor

  • EV Charger Installation: As Ann Arbor leads Michigan in electric vehicle adoption, we're your certified installer for Tesla Wall Connectors, ChargePoint, and all major EV charging brands. We handle DTE rebate paperwork and ensure your installation meets city requirements.
  • Panel Upgrades: Many Ann Arbor homes, especially in Water Hill, Kerrytown, and the Old Fourth Ward, have outdated electrical panels. We upgrade 100-amp services to 200-amp to support modern electrical demands and EV charging.
  • Whole House Generators: Protect your Ann Arbor home from power outages caused by storms and grid issues. As a Generac authorized dealer, we install automatic backup generators that keep your power on when DTE's grid fails.
  • Residential Electrical: Complete electrical services including outlet installation, circuit additions, lighting upgrades, and troubleshooting for Ann Arbor homes.
