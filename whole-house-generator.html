Whole House Generators

Never lose power again - automatic backup for your entire home

Power Outages Suck. We Can Fix That.

Remember the last time your power went out? Throwing away everything in the fridge, no heat in winter, no AC in summer. A whole house generator ends all that. Power goes out, generator kicks on in 10 seconds. You might not even notice.

How It Works (Simple Version)

1. Power goes out
2. Generator senses it and starts automatically
3. Your house switches to generator power in 10-20 seconds
4. Power comes back, generator shuts off
5. You didn't have to do anything

What Size Do You Need?

Forget the complicated calculations. Here's what works:

  • Small house (under 2,000 sq ft): 16-20kW generator
  • Average house (2,000-3,000 sq ft): 22kW generator
  • Large house (over 3,000 sq ft): 24-26kW generator
  • Have a hot tub or pool? Go up one size

The Real Costs

Generator installation in Washtenaw County runs $8,000-15,000 total. Here's what you're paying for:

  • Generator itself: $4,000-8,000
  • Automatic transfer switch: $1,500-2,000
  • Installation and wiring: $2,000-3,500
  • Gas line connection: $500-1,500
  • Concrete pad and permits: $500-1,000

Natural Gas vs Propane

Natural Gas: If you have it, use it. Unlimited runtime, no refills needed.
Propane: Need a big tank (500+ gallons). Works great but needs refills during extended outages.

Installation Process

Takes about 2-3 weeks start to finish:

  1. Site visit: We figure out the best location
  2. Permits: We handle all paperwork
  3. Schedule utilities: Gas company needs to run a line
  4. Pour pad: Concrete base for generator
  5. Install: Generator, transfer switch, wiring
  6. Test: Make sure everything works perfectly

What About Solar + Battery?

Great question. Solar + battery backup costs 3-4x more than a generator and won't run your AC or heat for days. Generators are still the best solution for extended outages. You can always add solar later.

Common Questions

What about HOAs?
Most HOAs allow generators. We'll help with the approval process.
