About Bremray Electrical

Family-owned electrical contractor serving Ann Arbor since 2004

Your Trusted Washtenaw County Electricians

Bremray Electrical has been in business since 1988, and we've proudly served Ann Arbor and Washtenaw County since 2004. As a family-owned business with 4 trucks and 2 dedicated teams, we're large enough to handle any project but small enough to care about every customer.

Why Choose Bremray?

  • Family-Owned Since 1988: Over 35 years of electrical expertise
  • Local Since 2004: Deep roots in the Ann Arbor community
  • Licensed & Insured: Full protection for your home and our work
  • Honest Pricing: Upfront quotes with no surprises
  • Quality Guarantee: We stand behind every job

Our Approach

We believe in solving problems, not just selling services. When you call Bremray, you'll speak directly with an electrician who can answer your questions honestly. We'll tell you what you need - and what you don't. That straightforward approach has earned us loyal customers throughout Ann Arbor, Ypsilanti, Chelsea, Saline, and Dexter.

What We Do Best

  • Panel Upgrades: Our specialty - from Federal Pacific replacements to 200-amp upgrades
  • EV Charger Installation: Certified installers for all major brands
  • Whole House Generators: Authorized Generac dealer and installer
  • Residential Service: Everything from outlets to complete rewiring

Our Team

With 4 trucks and 2 dedicated teams, we can respond quickly while maintaining the personal touch of a family business. Our electricians are experienced professionals who take pride in clean, code-compliant work. We're not the biggest electrical contractor in Washtenaw County, and that's by choice - we prefer building relationships over building volume.

Community Commitment

Living and working in Washtenaw County for over 20 years means we're your neighbors, not just your electricians. We shop where you shop, our kids go to school with your kids, and we care about the community we share. That's why we focus on honest advice and quality work - our reputation depends on it.
