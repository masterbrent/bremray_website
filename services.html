Panel Upgrades

Older homes often need panel upgrades to support modern electrical demands including EV charging, air conditioning, and home offices.

We upgrade panels when:

  • You're adding major loads (EV charger, hot tub, addition)
  • You have Federal Pacific or Zinsco (fire hazards)
  • Breakers trip constantly
  • Insurance company demands it
  • Still running on fuses

Typical upgrade includes: New 200-amp panel, updated grounding and bonding to current code, service disconnects, surge protection, and inspection.

Backup Power Solutions

Michigan weather causes regular power outages. Ice storms, wind storms, vehicle accidents. We offer two backup power options to fit different budgets and needs.

Portable generator with interlock kit:

  • Professional interlock installation: $1,200-1,500
  • Works with your existing generator
  • Or we supply tri-fuel generators ($900-1,500)
  • 50-amp models power all lights and outlets
  • Same-day installation

Automatic standby generators ($12,000+):

  • 22kW Generac systems (most common)
  • Automatic operation - no intervention needed
  • Natural gas or propane connection
  • Powers entire home
  • 2-day installation once permitted
